FAQ

What data do I need to provide?

Start with the clubs you play, on-course tendencies, and goals. A down-the-line swing video plus launch monitor snapshot (ball speed, launch, spin) helps Alex build faster.

How fast will I get my recommendations?

Most remote build sheets land in your inbox within one business day. If we need extra footage or numbers, we’ll reach out right away so nothing stalls.

Is the fitting really free?

Yes. The remote spec sheet and follow-up review are complimentary. You only pay if you want GolfMax to source and assemble the build.

Can I order clubs through GolfMax?

Absolutely. We’ll quote the winning build, dial in loft/lie and swingweight, and ship it—or hand you a parts list for your preferred shop.
