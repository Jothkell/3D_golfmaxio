How it works

1) Tell us about your game

Answer a quick questionnaire (handicap, ball speed, miss pattern) and optionally upload swing clips or launch monitor numbers.

2) We analyze & build your spec

GolfMax fitters review your data and prepare a precise spec sheet: head, loft, lie, shaft, length, grip, and gapping.

3) Get recommendations

Receive a shareable PDF with Good/Better/Best options. Buy anywhere or shop through GolfMax for assembly, pricing, and support.

FAQ

What data do I need to provide?

At minimum: typical carry distance, common miss, and goals. Helpful: ball speed, spin, launch, and 6–10 swings on video.

How fast do I get recommendations?

Most fittings are returned within ~24 hours (business days). We’ll email you the PDF spec sheet.

Do I have to buy from GolfMax?

No. You can purchase anywhere. If you buy through GolfMax, we’ll assemble and support the build with your exact specs.

Can I follow up with a fitter?

Yes—reply to your fitting email or book time at GolfMax. Your remote data carries over to in‑person sessions.
